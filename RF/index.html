Welcome to CVM University


CVM University, located in Vallabh Vidyanagar,

Gujarat, is a multi-faculty institution established in 2019 under the Charutar Vidya Mandal Trust.

It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines like Engineering, Science, Commerce, Law, Management, and more.

The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and emphasizes research and innovation.
